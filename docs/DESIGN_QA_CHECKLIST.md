# Affiliate Page Design QA Checklist

A public affiliate landing page is not ready unless it passes these checks.

## Visual Quality

- Page has a clean centered layout.
- Text is easy to read on desktop and mobile.
- Headline is clear and not hype-heavy.
- CTA button is obvious but not scammy.
- Disclosure is visible and easy to understand.
- Page does not look like raw/default HTML.
- Page has enough spacing between sections.

## Trust Quality

- No fake income claims.
- No fake review claims.
- No unsupported "best" claims.
- Affiliate/referral relationship is disclosed.
- Product explanation is useful even if the visitor does not click.
- Limitations or cautions are included when appropriate.

## Mobile Quality

- Page fits on phone screens.
- CTA button is easy to tap.
- Text does not overflow.
- Sections are not cramped.

## Publishing Rule

Do not send social traffic to a campaign page until the page passes design QA and technical QA.
